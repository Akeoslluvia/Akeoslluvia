Sailors Plate

Jeremy Santos a resilient individual who traversed the seas as a seafarer to eventually establish a successful venture in the fast-food industry. Through dedication, adaptability, and entrepreneurial spirit, Jeremy Santos transformed his life trajectory, embodying the essence of perseverance and ambition. Jeremy Santos and his wife are bartenders on a cruise ship, but the pandemic happened, So Jeremy Santos and his wife thought it would be a good idea to build our own business since we are also getting closer to our retirement age. first, we tried to frame a milk tea store; it was nice and stable, but we wanted to try to make our own milk tea store.Next, we want to try a barber shop because it is a low-cost business. and after months, we opened our sizzling restaurant, the Sailors Plate. With the help of my son and wife, we had a successful opening and a stable business.

Sailors Plate is more than just a place to grab a quick bite—we're your neighborhood hangout, your go-to spot for delicious food, and your home away from home. It all started during the pandemic. Like many,They found themselves facing uncertainty and challenges they never imagined. But instead of letting them down, Jeremy’s family decided to band together and do something positive for our community. When it comes to our food, they don't mess around. They use the freshest ingredients and time-tested recipes to create dishes that'll have your taste buds dancing. From juicy Sisig and mouthwatering Tapa to decadent desserts, they got something for everyone. But more than just serving up delicious food, Sailors Plate is all about making connections. Whether you're dining solo, catching up with friends, or bringing the whole family along, you'll always find a friendly face and a warm welcome here at Sailors Plate.

They are excited to continue serving their community and spreading a little joy wherever we go. So come to Sailor’s Plate, pull up a chair, and join us for a meal. They can’t wait to meet you!
